BACKSTORY

IN THE SHADOWED REALM OF ELDORIA, THERE LIES A FABLED LABYRINTH, CRAFTED BY ANCIENT MAGIC. AT ITS CENTER RESTS THE HEART-STONE, A POWERFUL RELIC GRANTING A SINGLE WISH TO ANY WHO REACH IT. COUNTLESS SOULS HAVE ENTERED ITS TWISTED HALLS, FACING SPECTRAL GUARDIANS AND ILLUSIONS, BUT NONE HAVE RETURNED. NOW, UNDER A MOONLIT SKY, A LONE ADVENTURER STEPS FORWARD, TORCH IN HAND, DETERMINED TO FIND THE HEART-STONE BEFORE THE LABYRINTH CLAIMS ANOTHER SOUL.
